MAGGI NOODLES

by gursangat

This is my favourite food because it can be made very easily and with much ease and no time and is a hunger burster!!! this can be made in a hostel room too so i personally enjoy it!!!

INGREDIENTS

  • MAGGI Noodles (Masala)
  • Oil tablespoon
  • Garlic 6 cloves
  • Black Cardamom
  • Carrot
  • Broccoli
  • Corn
  • water
  • Salt

RECIPE

  1. Cook Maggi Noodles according to package directions without the tastemaker.
  2. Drain and set aside.
    3. 🍝
  3. Heat oil in a pan and add garlic.
  4. Saute for a minute and add sos.
    5. 🍜
  5. Cook the mushrooms for two minutes and add pasta sauce, oregano and maggi noodles.🍴
  6. Bring this to a quick simmer and switch off the flame.
    7. 🥡
  7. Top with olives, basil and parmesan.
    8. 🥧
  8. Serve immediately.
    9. 🍸
